Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Premium Pastries

Orders & Payment

How do I place an order?
Simply browse our menu, add items to your cart, fill in your details, and click "Confirm Order via WhatsApp". Your order will be sent directly to our team via WhatsApp, and we'll confirm within 30 minutes.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash on delivery, bank transfer, and POS payments on delivery. For bank transfers, you'll receive our account details in the WhatsApp message.
Is there a minimum order amount?
No minimum order for pickup. For delivery, minimum order is ₦3,000. Catering orders start at ₦25,000.
Can I modify or cancel my order?
Yes, please contact us immediately via WhatsApp. Modifications are possible up to 1 hour before your selected pickup/delivery time. Cancellations made less than 1 hour before may incur a small fee.

Delivery & Pickup

How much is delivery?
Delivery fee is ₦1,500 within Abakaliki. We offer FREE delivery on all orders above ₦15,000 within Abakaliki.
What areas do you deliver to?
We deliver to all Southeast region. Check our Delivery Areas page for full list.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery typically takes 1hr-1day depending your location. We'll share a tracking link via WhatsApp when your order is out for delivery.
Can I pick up my order?
Absolutely! Our bakery is located at 10 Marcel Eze Street, Abakaliki. Orders are usually ready within 45 minutes for pickup. Hours: Mon-Sat 7am-7pm, Sun 9am-4pm.

Products & Ingredients

Are your pastries baked fresh daily?
Yes! All our pastries are baked fresh every morning. We don't sell day-old products. What you don't see on the menu is what's already sold out!
Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?
We currently offer limited vegan options (our chin chin and some breads). Gluten-free options are available upon request for custom orders with 48 hours notice.
Do you use preservatives?
No. We use only natural ingredients with no artificial preservatives. That's why our pastries are best consumed within 24-48 hours.
Can I order custom cakes?
Yes! We specialize in custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, and corporate events. Please contact us at least 7 days in advance. Prices start at ₦25,000.
What are your most popular items?
Our Classic Meat Pie, Butter Chin Chin, and Golden Puff Puff are customer favorites. The Red Velvet Cake is also extremely popular for celebrations!

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Reach out to us directly and we'll get back to you within 30 minutes.
