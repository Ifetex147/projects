Important Allergy Notice

Our kitchen handles dairy, eggs, wheat, nuts, and gluten. While we take precautions, cross-contamination may occur. Please inform us of severe allergies when ordering.

How to Order with Allergies

  1. Add items to your cart normally
  2. In the cart, find the "Special Instructions" box
  3. Clearly state your allergy and request (e.g., "No nuts, severe allergy")
  4. Proceed to WhatsApp and send your order
  5. We will confirm if we can accommodate your request

Pro tip: For severe allergies, call or WhatsApp us directly for the safest ordering experience.
