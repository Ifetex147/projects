Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order for events?

For custom cakes and large catering orders, please order at least 3 days in advance. For regular orders, 24 hours notice is appreciated but not required.

Do you deliver outside Lagos?

Currently, we only deliver within southeast region . You can always pick up from our Abakaliki bakery.

What if I have allergies?

Please add your allergy information in the "Special Instructions" box when ordering. Our kitchen handles nuts, dairy, and gluten.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Yes, please contact us immediately via WhatsApp. Modifications are possible up to 1 hour before your selected pickup/delivery time.
